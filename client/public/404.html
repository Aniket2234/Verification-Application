Backend Not Configured

Your frontend is trying to make API calls, but no backend URL is configured.

Quick Fix:

  1. Deploy your backend to Railway, Render, or Vercel
  2. Get your backend URL (e.g., https://your-app.up.railway.app)
  3. In Netlify: Site Settings → Environment Variables
  4. Add: VITE_API_URL = https://your-backend-url.com
  5. Redeploy your site
